About Dirty to Clean

Transforming dirty cars into shining masterpieces since 2015

We're more than just a car wash service. We're a team of passionate professionals dedicated to bringing sparkle back to your vehicle.

Our Story

It all started in 2015 when our founder, Alex Johnson, noticed how difficult it was to find reliable, high-quality car cleaning services. Tired of subpar experiences and inconsistent results, he decided to create something better.

What began as a single van with a dream has grown into a network of professional service providers across multiple cities. We've served over 50,000 customers and completed more than 100,000 car cleaning sessions, all while maintaining our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Today, Dirty to Clean stands as a testament to what happens when passion meets professionalism. We continue to innovate, improve, and expand our services while staying true to our core values.

"Our mission is simple: deliver exceptional car cleaning services that exceed expectations, every single time."

Alex Johnson
Founder & CEO

Our Journey

Milestones along our path to excellence

2015

The Beginning

Dirty to Clean was founded with a single van and a dream to revolutionize car cleaning services. Our first customer was a neighbor who needed an emergency car wash.

2017

Expansion & Technology

Launched our mobile app and website, making booking easier than ever. Expanded to three service vans and hired our first five employees.

2019

City Expansion

Expanded services to three major cities. Reached 10,000 customers milestone and introduced eco-friendly cleaning products across all services.

2021

Innovation Award

Received the "Innovation in Service Industry" award. Launched our subscription model and introduced AI-powered scheduling optimization.

2023

National Recognition

Featured in major business publications. Expanded to 10 cities nationwide and crossed 50,000 customers served.

2024

The Future

Currently working on electric vehicle specialty services and planning international expansion. Our journey continues with the same passion and commitment to excellence.
